COMPANY PURCHASING POLICY - VER 2025.1

1. SPENDING LIMITS:
- Any single purchase exceeding $5,000 requires a documented Pre-Approval Form.
- Purchases under $500 can be approved by department heads.

2. VENDOR REQUIREMENTS:
- IT Equipment (Laptops, Monitors, Servers) must be purchased from "Tech Solutions Inc." or "Global IT Corp."
- Software licenses must be verified for security compliance.

3. DOCUMENTATION:
- Travel expenses (Flights, Hotels) must include a "Business Justification" note.
- Consulting fees exceeding $10,000 require a signed Statement of Work (SOW).

4. CATEGORIZATION:
- All invoices must be assigned one of the 20 corporate spending categories.
